Welcome to Liosbeag
Liosbeag lies in the small village of Bhaltos amidst some of the most stunning scenery in the Outer Hebrides.

Self Contained Modern Cottage

Liosbeag is a self contained modern cottage with its own garden – a 'work in progress' - and wonderful views of sea and beach.

Uig Peninsula

The area, which is part of the Uig Peninsula is community owned and run by a Trust. It is well known for its beautiful beaches, wildlife and outdoor activities in general.

Eco Heating

It has an open plan design, both comfortable and practical for easy living with a wood burning stove and exhaust air heating system which heats radiators and supplies hot water.

Travel

By air to Stornoway then car hire or post bus to Uig. Car Ferries sail between Ullapool and Stornoway and Uig, Skye and Tarbert, Harris every day in the summer. There is also a connecting ferry between Leverburgh, Harris and to the Uists and Benbecula.

Renting

The weekly rental – this varies depending on time of year – includes towels, bed linen and in the summer £20 of electricity. Also included is a pack of wood bricks and kindling. The normal changeover day is Saturday noon but any variations can usually be accommodated. A 20% deposit is required on booking with the remainder payable 6 weeks prior to your holiday.
